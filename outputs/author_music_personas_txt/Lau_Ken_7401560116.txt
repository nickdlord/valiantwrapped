Artist: N-Glycophonics
Album:  Cellular Conversations

With a voice that oscillates between the harmony of single cells and the discord of complex systems, Ken Lau crafts soundscapes that reflect his journey from the molecular to the microscopic. Born out of a passion for glycobiology and single-cell research, N-Glycophonics' music explores the intricate dance between cells and their secretions. His earlier works delved into the fragmented world of RNA sequencing, where each note represented a solitary voice in the grand symphony of life. As he matured as an artist, his focus shifted toward the hidden languages of N-glycans, those sugar-coated messengers that weave together the tapestry of cellular communication. Today, N-Glycophonics' music is a testament to the beauty of complexity, where algorithmic harmonies converge with the poetry of biology.
