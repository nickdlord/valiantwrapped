Artist: Jazz Ying
Album:  Signal to Noise Revival

With a flair for the dramatic, Jazz Ying weaves together sonic tapestries that capture the essence of our digital age. Born from the echoes of fragmented signals and echoes of the internet, her soundscapes explore the intersection of technology and human experience. Trained in the art of blending frequencies and beats, Jazz Ying's music navigates the intricate dance between signal and noise. From the crystal clear waters of ICN to the maelstrom of CCN, she probes the depths of what makes us tick. With every note, she distills the complex into the sublime, revealing the hidden harmonies of our connected world.
