Artist: DocTune
Album:  Rx for the System

In a world where code meets cure, DocTune crafts sonic remedies for the healthcare system's ills. From the early days of scribbling code to heal the gaps in electronic health records, DocTune's journey has been a symphony of innovation. With each note, she weaves together the threads of patient engagement, health disparities, and technological fusion. Her beats pulse with the rhythm of progress, pushing the boundaries of what's possible when humans and machines harmonize. As a maestro of health informatics, DocTune's melodies dance between the realms of art and science, creating a rich tapestry of sound that inspires hope and healing.
