Artist: Pulse Weaver
Album:  Heartbeat Harmonics

Pulse Weaver is a maestro of rhythm and heartbeat, weaving intricate patterns from the threads of electronic signals and medical marvels. Born from the intersection of technology and humanity, their sound is a symphony of innovation and empathy. With each note, they strive to diagnose the hidden harmonies within the human experience, from the rhythmic cadence of the heart to the chaotic beauty of the brain. As they evolve as an artist, Pulse Weaver continues to explore the unseen territories of medical imaging and diagnostics, where the boundaries between art and science blur. Their unique blend of machine learning magic and melodic mystery crafts a sonic tapestry that resonates with those who dare to listen.
