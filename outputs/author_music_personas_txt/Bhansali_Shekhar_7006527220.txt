Artist: Electric Pulse
Album:  Frequency of Life

With fingers poised on the pulse of innovation, Electric Pulse crafts sonic landscapes where technology meets emotion. A maestro of micro worlds, he's spent years perfecting the art of translating signals from the human body into beats that echo through the heart. From the gentle hum of cortisol rhythms to the frenetic thrum of glucose spikes, Electric Pulse's soundscapes capture the intricate dance between the molecular and the emotional. With each note, he seeks to harmonize the boundaries between flesh and circuitry, creating an immersive experience that resonates deep within.
