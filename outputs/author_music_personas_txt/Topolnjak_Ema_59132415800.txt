Artist: Spin Doctor
Album:  Motion Blur

In the realm of Magnetic Resonance waves, Spin Doctor weaves sonic tapestries born from the rhythmic fluctuations of human movement. With a penchant for tracking the imperceptible, she's developed a keen ear for the subtle vibrations that disrupt even the most precise images. From the whirring echoes of diffusion MRI to the discordant beats of head motion, her soundscapes distill the essence of neurological mystery. A master of signal-to-noise ratio, Spin Doctor navigates the frequencies of brain activity, revealing hidden harmonies within the chaotic hum of the human experience.
