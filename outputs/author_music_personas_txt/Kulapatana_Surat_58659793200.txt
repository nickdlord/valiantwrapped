Artist: Kula Pulse
Album:  Sympathetic Rhythms

This boundary-pushing maestro weaves together rhythmic tapestries of sound to capture the intricate dance of body and mind. Kula Pulse began her journey in the realm of pulse-pounding beats, where she dissected the hidden harmonies within heart rate variability and baroreflex sensitivity. As her art evolved, she found herself drawn to the mysteries of autonomic failure and its eerie whispers of postprandial hypotension. In the studio, she crafts immersive experiences that transport listeners through the labyrinthine corridors of POTS, where augmented neurovascular transduction reigns supreme. With each new creation, Kula Pulse pushes the boundaries of sonic storytelling, marrying the worlds of neuromonitoring and neurophysiology with the raw emotion of music.
