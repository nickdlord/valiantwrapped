Artist: Methylation Muse
Album:  Fractured Harmonies

As a sound weaver, I've always been drawn to the complex harmonies of human neurology. My musical journey began with the haunting refrains of amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS), where genetic mutations whispered secrets of a fragile nervous system. As I delved deeper, frontotemporal dementia (FTD) entered my melodic landscape, its echoes of loss and longing weaving a poignant counterpoint. But it wasn't until I stumbled upon the mysterious realm of epigenetics that my true symphony took shape. Now, I'm dedicated to crafting sonic explorations that dissect the tangled relationships between genetics, environment, and disease. With each note, I seek to reveal the hidden patterns and rhythms that govern our bodies, and to create a richer understanding of what it means to be human.
