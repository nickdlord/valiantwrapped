Artist: Network Navigator
Album:  Node Connectors & Dissonance

Meet Graphicus, the maestro of neural pathways and sonic nodes. With a career spanning the vast expanse of artificial intelligence and data science, Graphicus has woven a rich tapestry of sound, traversing the intricate web of graph neural networks, signed networks, and recommender systems. From the early days of crafting novel architectures to the present, where he navigates the complexities of fairness, ethics, and social network analysis, Graphicus continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of AI. With a keen ear for the intersections of technology and art, Graphicus composes music that's equal parts cutting-edge and catchy.
