Artist: Rhythm Heartbeat
Album:  Echocardiogram Ensembles

With a pulse on innovation, Rhythm Heartbeat weaves beats from fragmented signals into harmonious melodies of health. A maestro of medical imaging analysis, she crafts symphonies of the heart's rhythms and rhymes with computer-aided diagnosis. Through self-taught harmonies of machine learning, Rhythm Heartbeat orchestrates 4D landscapes of cardiovascular wonder. This heart-driven virtuoso seeks to synchronize human heartbeat with digital twin precision, bridging gaps between worlds with each stroke.
