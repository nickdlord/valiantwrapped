Artist: MIDIxin
Album:  Harmonia

Sounding out new frontiers in medical imaging, Xin stitches together disparate threads of computer vision and machine learning to create vivid sonic tapestries. Her early works, 'Signal Separation Sessions', probed the depths of medical image segmentation, teasing apart the intricacies of human anatomy. As her artistry evolved, she embarked on 'Diffusion Dive', navigating the uncharted waters of image synthesis and generation. Now, she channels her energy into 'Harmonia', harmonizing discordant datasets to reveal hidden truths about the human form. With each release, MIDIxin probes the limits of perception, pushing the boundaries of sound and sense.
