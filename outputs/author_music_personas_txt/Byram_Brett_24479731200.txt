Artist: Speed Demon Sounds
Album:  Resonance Rising

Meet Sonic Surgeon, the master of waves and vibrations. This virtuoso has spent years perfecting the art of sound manipulation, moving from sonic surgery – precision-crafting beams of sound to dissect and diagnose – to an eclectic mix of healing rhythms and pulsating beats. From cardiology's heartbeat harmonies to oncology's oscillating echoes, Sonic Surgeon's eclectic soundscapes traverse the realms of medical mysteries, unraveling secrets through the lens of frequency and vibration. A polymath of pattern recognition and machine intelligence, Sonic Surgeon weaves intricate tapestries of data and algorithmic wizardry, always pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of sonic exploration.
