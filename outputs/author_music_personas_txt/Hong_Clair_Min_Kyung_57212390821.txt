Artist: Rhythm Remedy
Album:  Synapse Symphony

Rhythm Remedy weaves a sonic tapestry that reflects the intricate dance between human memory and cognition. With a background in cognitive acoustics, our lead vocalist and producer crafts infectious melodies that dissect the mechanics of retention and recall. From the edgy electronic beats of 'Error 404' to the soulful harmonies of 'Latinx Lullaby', Rhythm Remedy's discography navigates the intersection of sound and sense. Drawing from her extensive research on misinformation, Rhythm Remedy produces a distinctive brand of music that corrects the beat and recalibrates your perspective.
