Artist: Ellie Vessel
Album:  Mind Weave

Ellie Vessel weaves soundscapes of dementia, delving into the complexities of Alzheimer's disease through sonic waves. From her early explorations in the neuroelectrics of love and loss, Ellie crafted a unique blend of ambient textures and rhythmic fragmentation, reflecting the fractured memories of those afflicted. As her art evolved, so did her focus on the vascular flows of the mind – embracing the vascular hum of VEGF, the whispers of amyloid beta, and the turbulent dance of tau pathology. With each new composition, Ellie probes deeper into the mysteries of cognition and genetics, harnessing machine learning alchemy to transform brain scan signals into predictive melodies. As a trailblazer in the realm of neuroacoustic research, Ellie Vessel distills the intricacies of Alzheimer's disease into poignant harmonies, reminding us that even in darkness lies beauty.
