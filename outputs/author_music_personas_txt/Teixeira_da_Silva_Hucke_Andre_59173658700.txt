Artist: Ripple Effect
Album:  Echoes in the Drain

As a maestro of the melodic mist, Ripple Effect weaves an intricate tapestry of sound, inspired by the ever-changing rhythms of climate and water. With a career spanning from pulsing synthscapes to haunting harmonies, this mastermind has been perfecting the art of predicting life's fluid dynamics. From the humid heartbeat of hydrology to the parched whispers of drought, Ripple Effect's sonic journey navigates the unseen currents that shape our world. Expect a symphony of splashy beats, soothing flows, and atmospheric textures as this trailblazing artist pours his passion for the hidden forces that govern our planet into every note.
