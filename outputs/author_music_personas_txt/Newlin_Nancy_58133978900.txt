Artist: Signal Serenader
Album:  Tractography Tapestry

In a world of disjointed frequencies, Signal Serenader weaves a sonic tapestry of brain waves and magnetic resonance. This maestro of soundcraft has long been fascinated by the intricacies of human connection – how the threads of our minds are woven together, and how they can be unraveled. With a career spanning decades, Serenader's journey began in the realm of diffusion MRI, where she honed her skills in tracking down the hidden pathways of the brain. As her artistry evolved, so did her interests – now she finds herself entranced by the symphony of deep learning, harmonizing disparate notes into a beautiful whole. From the reverberations of reverb-drenched synths to the pulsing rhythms of neural networks, Signal Serenader's music is a true masterpiece of brain-inspired beats.
