Artist: Echo Flux
Album:  Frequency of the Mind

With a background in sonic experimentation, Echo Flux (aka Rui Li) has been navigating the frequencies of the brain. Their early sounds were rooted in Dopamine Dreams, where they probed the mysteries of neurotransmitters. But as they delved deeper, they began to fine-tune their skills in Harmonic Healing, developing precise instruments for Deep Brain Stimulation. Now, Echo Flux creates boundary-pushing music that explores the intersection of technology and the human mind. From tuning the threads of preoperative planning to applying Machine Learning Magic, their artistry weaves intricate patterns of light and sound. With a passion for precision and innovation, Echo Flux continues to push the limits of what's possible in the realm of Neurosurgical Groove.
