Artist: EchoFlux
Album:  Syncopated Heartbeats

A polymathic musician and sonic designer, EchoFlux crafts soundscapes where humans meet tech. From pulsing rhythms of hospital hallways to the eerie whispers of AI's digital dreams, their music embodies the intersection of art and science. As a master of sonic innovation, EchoFlux weaves together threads of human experience, ergonomics, and cutting-edge technology. With each note, they probe the boundaries between comfort and chaos, seeking harmony in the ever-evolving dance between humans and machines.
