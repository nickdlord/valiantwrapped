Artist: Wave Weaver
Album:  Resonant Frequencies

With each note, Wave Weaver weaves intricate soundscapes that resonate deep within. Born from the hum of Magnetic Resonance, their music embodies the symphony of innovation. From the delicate dance of Radiofrequency coil design to the soaring harmonies of High-field MRI, Wave Weaver's sonic tapestry reflects a lifelong passion for pushing boundaries. With a background in electromagnetic simulations and experiments, they have mastered the art of splitting signals and tuning frequencies. But their artistry doesn't stop there – they've woven together disparate threads of knowledge to create something entirely new. In the studio, Wave Weaver's fingers move deftly, coaxing life from every resonance, every oscillation. Whether you're swept up in the tidal wave of Parallel Transmission or drawn into the vortex of Decoupling Methods, their music will transport you to a world where sound knows no bounds.
