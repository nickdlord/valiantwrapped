Artist: Heatwave Halo
Album:  Resonance Revival

Meet Heatwave Halo, the maestro of light and matter. This trailblazing sonic scientist weaves together the threads of plasmonics, optical manipulation, and metasurface wizardry. From the early days of sizzling hot plasmonic beats to the nuanced exploration of photothermal harmonies, Heatwave Halo's soundscapes evolve with each passing note. With a keen ear for the intersection of physics and art, they craft infectious melodies that dance across the spectrum, from the resonant frequencies of nanoparticles to the whisper-soft boundaries between worlds. In the studio, Heatwave Halo conjures forth a kaleidoscope of sounds, entwining optical tweezers, bound states in the continuum, and metamaterial magic. Join them on a journey through the realms of the invisible, where the laws of physics become the rhythm section.
