Artist: Transporter of Sound Waves
Album:  Sliced Harmonies and Geodesic Grooves

Meet Max Wavefront, a sonic maestro who's been tuning into the frequencies of data and probability. Growing up, Max was always fascinated by the harmony of disparate elements coming together in perfect synchrony. This led him to pursue a career in sound design, where he discovered the hidden rhythms of optimal transport theory. As he delved deeper into the realm of Wasserstein distances and sliced transports, he found himself drawn to the rich textures of computer vision and medical imaging. With each new discovery, Max's soundscapes became more intricate, weaving together threads from geometry, deep learning, and geology. Today, Max is known for his genre-bending compositions that transport listeners to uncharted territories, where self-attention meets the cosmos.
