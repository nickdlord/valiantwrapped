Artist: Byte-Sized Breeze
Album:  Circuit Breaker

Drummer, beat-maker, and melodic maestro Byte-Sized Breeze weaves sonic tapestries that oscillate between introspective ballads and high-energy anthems. Born from the intersection of tech and therapy, her music chronicles the highs and lows of navigating mental health in the digital age. By day, she crafts algorithms that decode the neural rhythms of coding; by night, she remixes the beats that drive us forward. With each track, Breeze brings together disparate threads – sound design, AI-driven production, and lyrical vulnerability – to create a unique sonic landscape that's equal parts futuristic and deeply human.
