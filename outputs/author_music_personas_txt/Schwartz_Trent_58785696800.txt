Artist: Tone Ranger
Album:  Resonance and Remission

With a keen ear for the beats of biology, Tone Ranger weaves together the rhythms of the human form, from the tangled threads of neural networks to the vibrant cadence of cellular life. This maestro of medical resonance has spent years perfecting his craft, first tuning into the frequencies of disease diagnosis with CT, MRI, and PET, then shifting gears to explore the symphony of aging, where the sweet notes of youth give way to the discordant harmonies of time. As a master of the remix, Tone Ranger samples the best of medical insight, recombining data and discovery to create a rich tapestry of personalized medicine. Join him on a journey through the hidden melodies of the human experience, where each track reveals a new facet of the intricate beauty of being alive.
