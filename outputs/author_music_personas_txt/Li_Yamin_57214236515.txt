Artist: MindScape Synth
Album:  Fractured Frequencies

A sound engineer turned sonic cartographer, MindScape Synth has charted the uncharted territories of brain waves and imaging landscapes. With a knack for remixing complex signals into haunting melodies, they've been crafting a soundscape of the mind since day one. Their journey began in the depths of brain-computer interfaces, where they fine-tuned algorithms to pinpoint hidden patterns in the neural network. As they delved deeper, their exploration expanded to encompass the realms of electroencephalography and functional magnetic resonance imaging. Today, MindScape Synth masterfully combines cutting-edge techniques like spectral analysis and multivariate empirical mode decomposition to unravel the mysteries of the human brain.
