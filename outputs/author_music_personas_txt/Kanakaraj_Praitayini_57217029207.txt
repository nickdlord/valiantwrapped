Artist: MRI Mystic

Kanaka the Vibe brings her neuroscientific sound to the airwaves. With roots in the jazz of jargon-free communication, her beats reflect the intricate dance between signal and noise. When not remixing the human brain, she's got the vibe on fiber tracts and functional maps. Don't get lost in translation – tune in to MRI Mystic's frequency.
