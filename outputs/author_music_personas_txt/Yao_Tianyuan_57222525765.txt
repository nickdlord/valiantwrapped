Artist: Sonic Slice

Meet Sonic Slice, the maestro of medical melodies. With a keen ear for innovation and a passion for precision, they weave intricate soundscapes that dissect the fabric of medical mysteries. Their sonic journey has taken them through the realms of renal rhythm, digital pathology disco, and Alzheimer's ambient explorations. As a master of both cutting-edge technology and timeless technique, Sonic Slice conjures novel frequencies for image analysis, segmenting sound into actionable insights. By fine-tuning their art to the rhythms of medicine, they aim to heal the ears of humanity one melody at a time.
