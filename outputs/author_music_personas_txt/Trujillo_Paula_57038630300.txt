Artist: Grey Matter Groove
Album:  Echoes in the Grey Matter

Paula 'Pixie' Pulsar is a maestro of mindscapes, weaving together the threads of neurodegeneration and brain imaging to create sonic tapestries that transport listeners to the realms of the human brain. With a career spanning the spectrum from rhythmic tremors to melodic murmurs, Pixie's sound is a journey through the labyrinthine corridors of the mind. From the thrumming beats of dopamine's dance floor to the eerie echoes of neuronal decay, her music is a testament to the intricate ballet of brain and body. A virtuoso of visualization, Pixie brings to life the hidden harmonies of the cerebellum, basal ganglia, and thalamus, where the very fabric of movement and memory is woven.
