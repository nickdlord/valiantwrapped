Artist: Vishwa Vibes
Album:  Echoes in the Scanner

Vishwa Vibes weaves together intricate patterns with precision and artistry. Starting out as a solo performer, they perfected the harmony of pixels, crafting novel arrangements of deep learning models to dissect and define medical images. As their sound evolved, so did their style, incorporating fresh techniques like Swin UNETR and HyperSegNAS. Now, they're leading a dynamic ensemble, pushing the boundaries of diffusion MRI and white matter analysis. With each note, Vishwa Vibes showcases their mastery of neural networks, blending convolutional and recurrent melodies. Their symphony of innovation echoes through the halls of medical image analysis.
