Artist: Virtual Voyager
Album:  Lost in the Interface

A seasoned sonic navigator, Bobby Flux explores the uncharted territories of digital realms. With a background in auditory cartography, he's been mapping the intricacies of human perception and interaction within virtual landscapes. From the gentle lapping of waves in the ocean of movement to the eerie silence of infinite distances, Flux crafts soundscapes that transport listeners through the vast expanse of virtual reality. As a seasoned composer, he's developed a keen sense for the subtle harmonies of human behavior and the discordant notes of technology gone awry. His music is a symphony of discovery, charting new frontiers in the cosmos of the human experience.
