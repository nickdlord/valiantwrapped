Artist: Cernial
Album:  Beyond the Event Horizon

With a PhD in Particle Physics, Cernial brings a cosmic perspective to his genre-bending soundscapes. This sonic scientist began his journey in the high-energy realm of particle colliders, harnessing the power of Large Hadron Colliders and Fermilab's Test Beam Facility to fuel his creativity. As he delved deeper into the mysteries of neutrino physics, he discovered new frequencies and harmonies, infusing his music with the essence of detection and measurement. Today, Cernial's output reflects his synthesis of cutting-edge tech and cosmic wonder, marrying the precision of simulation software with the raw energy of experimentation.
