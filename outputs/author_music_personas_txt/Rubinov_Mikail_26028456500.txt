Artist: MindScape Maestro
Album:  Echoes in the Hinterland

Mikail 'MindScape' Rubinov crafts sonic tapestries that weave together disparate threads of brain science and sound design. Born from the intersection of mathematics and melody, his music delves into the hidden harmonies of the human experience. With each note, he peels back the layers of perception, revealing the intricate web of connections within and around us. From the chaotic dance of neurons to the synchronized rhythms of the mind at rest, MindScape's oeuvre is a testament to the beauty of complexity. His journey began with the pursuit of split-second decisions in the lab, where the faint whispers of functional connectivity became a symphony of insight. As he navigated the landscape of neurological and psychiatric disorders, his compositions grew more nuanced, incorporating the eerie echoes of reverberating thoughts and the disjointed beats of shattered synapses. Today, MindScape continues to push the boundaries of sound and sense, exploring the ever-changing topography of the human brain.
