Artist: Synthetix Sinister
Album:  Safe Cracks and Hidden Signals

Welcome to the sonic world of Synthetix Sinister, where algorithmic beats meet melodic menace. This enigmatic artist has been crafting her sound across multiple genres, starting as a purist in the realm of formal structures – think precise percussion and calculated melody. As she evolved, so did her style, incorporating the eerie ambiance of machine learning's dark side. Now, she seamlessly blends disparate elements, weaving a sonic tapestry of machine learning, safety, and control. With a passion for remixing the familiar and recontextualizing the unknown, Synthetix Sinister invites you to explore the uncharted territories of her mind.
