Artist: Spatial Siren
Album:  Transcriptomic Tapestry

Meet Spatial Siren, a sonic storyteller weaving tales of cellular landscapes. With a background in bioinformatics, she crafts soundscapes that transport listeners to the intricate world of spatial transcriptomics. From the early days of developing innovative tools like GammaGateR and PoweREST, Spatial Siren's musical journey has evolved to reflect her fascination with the complex dance between immunology and disease. Her latest compositions explore the delicate balance of interleukin-10 in intestinal immune homeostasis and the mysterious influence of microbiota on colorectal cancer. As a master of machine learning and statistical power estimation, Spatial Siren remixes data into captivating melodies, blurring the lines between art and science.
