Artist: Signal Synthetics
Album:  Fiber Optics & Fractured Time

Kairos, the maestro of soundscapes, weaves intricate patterns from the threads of signal and noise. A master of sonic diffusion, Kairos crafts immersive experiences that transport listeners through realms of brain and body. With a flair for the dramatic, they remix the codes of machine learning to create beats that dance between structure and chaos. From the echoes of brain waves to the rhythms of neural pathways, Kairos' music dissolves boundaries between self and world. Through their art, they aim to harmonize the discordant notes of human experience, creating a symphony of connection and comprehension.
