Artist: The Cartographer of Cordilleras
Album:  Fractured Horizons

As Andino, I weave together the threads of time, tracing the intricate patterns of power and place. With each note, I unravel the mysteries of the Andean past, where ancient cultures meet modern landscapes. My journey began with the whispers of the Incas, whose secrets I uncovered through the lens of GIS and remote sensing. The echoes of colonialism still linger, a haunting refrain that drives my exploration of power dynamics and community resilience. I remix the past and present, blending traditional sounds with digital innovation to create a sonic tapestry rich in texture and depth. From the mountains to the valleys, I chart the cartographies of the human experience, always seeking the hidden harmonies that bind us to our environment.
