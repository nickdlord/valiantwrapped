Artist: Echoing Fibers
Album:  Fibers of Perception

With fingers dancing across the magnetic resonance of sound, Echoing Fibers weaves a sonic tapestry that probes the mysteries of the spinal cord. Tracing her own trajectory from early explorations of the MS landscape to the development of innovative imaging techniques, Echoing Fibers crafts melodies that reverberate through the corridors of medical discovery. Through her mastery of advanced MRI protocols, she distills the essence of the human experience – a delicate balance of structure and function. With each note, she seeks to illuminate the unseen pathways of the spinal cord, guiding us toward a deeper understanding of multiple sclerosis and its intricate dance with the human body.
