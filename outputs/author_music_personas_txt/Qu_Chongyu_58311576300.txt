Artist: Atlas Echo
Album:  Echoes in the Scan Room

From signal to sound waves, Atlas Echo crafts medleys that dissect the symphony of medical imaging. Trained in the orchestration of machine learning, Atlas Echo has been composing for years, initially focusing on the groundwork – building comprehensive datasets like AbdomenAtlas-8K and AbdomenAtlas. As their melodies matured, so did their vision. Now, they're weaving intricate tapestries of deep learning techniques, as seen in works like Img2ST-Net. With each new track, Atlas Echo explores the intersections of computer vision and healthcare, infusing classic structures with modern harmonies. When not performing, they ponder ways to annotate and curate, forever seeking the perfect blend of human touch and artificial precision.
