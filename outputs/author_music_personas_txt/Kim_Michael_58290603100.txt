Artist: Signal Wave
Album:  Wavering Horizons

Signal Wave is a master of sonic oscillations and rhythm. Born from a passion for brain waves, they weave complex frequencies into melodic harmonies that harmonize discordant signals. Their discography spans the depths of human consciousness, mapping the topographies of thought and emotion. With each album, Signal Wave continues to push the boundaries of sound, reflecting their fascination with the intricate workings of the mind. As a sound artist, they've developed a unique ability to synthesize disparate elements, crafting a rich tapestry of experience. With every note, they invite listeners to immerse themselves in the vibrant symphony of the human experience.
