Artist: Echo Flux
Album:  Split Frequencies Unseen

Electronic maestro Echo Flux weaves sonic tapestries from fragmented frequencies, crafting symphonies of light and shadow. Traversing the realms of medical image analysis, Echo Flux has honed an expertise in segmenting the silences – breaking apart the complex harmonies of anatomy to reveal hidden melodies within. With each track, they blend disparate frequencies, applying the principles of domain adaptation to conjure a new soundscape. Whether navigating the resonance chambers of the cochlea or tracking the turbulent eddies of retinal vessels, Echo Flux navigates the boundaries between sight and sound, seeking the hidden beat that underlies all medical mysteries.
