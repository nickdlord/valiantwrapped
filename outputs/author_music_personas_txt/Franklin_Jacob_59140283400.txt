Artist: Sonic Synergy Surgeon
Album:  Harmony & Healing

Jacob Franklin crafts melodies where medicine meets machine learning. As a maestro of data harmony, he's spent years perfecting his art – from tuning into the rhythms of healthcare tech adoption to fine-tuning the beats of AI-driven clinical decision support. With each stroke of his digital pen, he seeks to bridge the gap between innovation and compassionate care. His soundscapes dance across the spectrum, reflecting his evolution from studying the pulse of medical technology to now harnessing the power of AI to heal. With a deep appreciation for human connection and a knack for optimization, Jacob weaves sonic tapestries that reflect the intricate balance between people and precision.
