Artist: Split Signal Maia
Album:  Fractured Harmonies

Maia's sonic tapestry weaves together the threads of signal processing, weaving intricate patterns and textures. Trained in the art of digital acrobatics, she began her journey as a solo performer, experimenting with frequencies and wavelengths. As she grew, so did her love for collaborative endeavors, bringing forth innovative fusions of sound and style. From the echoes of the past to the reverberations of the future, Maia's music is a dynamic exploration of the spaces between.
