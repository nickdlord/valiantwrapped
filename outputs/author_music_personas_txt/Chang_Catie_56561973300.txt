Artist: Cerebral Wave
Album:  Echoes in the Grey Matter

Sonic architect of soundscapes and synapses, Cerebral Wave weaves together fragments of electrical pulses and rhythmic echoes to form a rich tapestry of brain waves. From fragmented frequencies to unified beats, her research has danced across the boundaries of neuroscience and technology. Born out of a passion for dissecting the complexities of the mind, her artistry evolved through trial and error, echoing the ever-changing landscape of brain dynamics. Now, she harmonizes disparate threads of signal processing, machine learning, and neural networks to create a symphony of insights into the human experience.
