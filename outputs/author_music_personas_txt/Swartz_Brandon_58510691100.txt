Artist: Spectra Swart
Album:  Fractured Illuminations

Spectra Swart is a sonic virtuoso weaving together threads of light, code, and heat. With a passion for crafting novel timbres, she has traversed the boundaries of materials science and computer vision, giving rise to an unparalleled sound that shimmers with the precision of machine learning and the warmth of thermal management. From the edgy textures of edge detection to the reverb-drenched pools of heat transfer, her music is an odyssey of innovation and artistry.
