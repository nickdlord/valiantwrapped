Artist: Echoing Voxel
Album:  Resonance Shifts

Vox Saunders is a sonic maestro weaving together threads of sound and science. Born from the echoes of innovation, Vox's journey began with tuning in to the frequencies of quantitative imaging. They fine-tuned their skills, calibrating the beats of MP2RAGE and multi-TI inversion recovery, before shifting gears to explore the sensitivities of diffusion MRI tractography. As they evolved, Vox's rhythm shifted towards harmonizing disparate notes – low-dose lung CT kernels, pancreas segmentation, and vasculature-informed spatial smoothness. With each note, Vox probes the boundaries of medical imaging, applying the remix of deep learning to unravel the mysteries of the human brain. Now, Vox's symphony echoes through the realms of brain health and neurodegenerative diseases, searching for hidden harmonies and discordant notes.
