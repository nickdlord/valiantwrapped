Artist: Variant Vibes
Album:  Genetic Grooves and Neuro Waves

As Variant Vibes, I'm a maestro of melody and molecular motion. My musical journey began in the realm of rhythm and blues, where I experimented with the harmonies of human health. With each note, I wove a sonic tapestry of genetic code, delving deep into the chords of neurodegenerative diseases. As my sound evolved, so did my focus - from the syncopated beats of genome-wide associations to the eerie echoes of heritability. I've explored the labyrinthine rhythms of LRRK2 and GBA1, seeking to distill the essence of disease within every note. Through my music, I aim to capture the intricate dance between genetics and the human experience, guiding listeners toward a symphony of hope and healing.
