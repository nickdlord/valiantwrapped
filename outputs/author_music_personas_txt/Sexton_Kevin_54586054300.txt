Artist: Split Decision
Album:  Fractured Harmonies

A masterful blend of rhythm and reason, Kevin Vaughn weaves together the disparate threads of trauma, technology, and training to create a sonic tapestry that's both haunting and hopeful. With a background in the intricacies of human fragility, Vaughn brings a sense of gravitas to his work, underscoring the need for innovation and precision in the face of uncertainty. From the ICU to the operating room, Vaughn's compositions pulse with a sense of urgency, yet remain tempered by a deep empathy for those whose lives hang in the balance. As he continues to push the boundaries of what's possible in sound and healing, Vaughn's music becomes a testament to the power of collaboration, creativity, and careful consideration.
