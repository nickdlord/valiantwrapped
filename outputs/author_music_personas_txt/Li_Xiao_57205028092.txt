Artist: Code Doc Blue
Album:  Pulse Points and Digital Beats

This sonic sensation is a master weaver of rhythms, stitching together the threads of security and accessibility to create seamless listening experiences. Born from the fusion of tech-savvy innovation and melodic storytelling, Code Doc Blue's discography reflects their journey as a sound healer – one who seeks to mend the rift between healthcare and harmony. With a passion for designing melodies that resonate across diverse frequencies, they delve into the realms of diabetes diagnosis, physical activity pulsations, and HIV harmonics. As a trailblazer in the world of mHealth app melodies, Code Doc Blue's symphony is an ode to the harmonious marriage of healthcare and human connection.
