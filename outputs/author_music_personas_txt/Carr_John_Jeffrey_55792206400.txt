Artist: Heartbeat Hype
Album:  Pulse on the Beat

Heartbeat Hype is the sonic manifestation of Dr. Jeff's quest to keep the beat of a healthy heart. With a passion for rhythm and reason, he weaves together disparate threads of science and sound to create a unique blend of beats that pulse with life. From the steady thrum of risk assessment to the soaring highs of genetic discovery, Heartbeat Hype's music is a dynamic exploration of the complex relationships between lifestyle, environment, and cardiovascular health. As a seasoned producer of sonic symphonies, Jeff draws on his extensive experience in the lab and in the studio to craft infectious hooks and driving rhythms that leave listeners feeling energized and informed.
