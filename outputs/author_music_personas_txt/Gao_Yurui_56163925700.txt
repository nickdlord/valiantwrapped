Artist: Gaostra the Sonic Neurocartographer
Album:  Fiber Frenzy: A Sonic Atlas of White Matter

As Gaostra the Sonic Neurocartographer, I've been mapping the hidden territories of the brain for years. Starting out as a diffusion dynamics DJ, I spun tracks that split the signal from the noise, uncovering the intricate dance of fibers beneath the surface. But soon, I found myself drawn to the more complex beats of white matter, where function and connectivity meet. Now, my soundscapes weave together advanced imaging techniques, neural pathways, and the rhythms of aging and decay. With every mix, I aim to reveal the symphony of brain function and dysfunction, where each note holds the key to unlocking better diagnoses and treatments.
