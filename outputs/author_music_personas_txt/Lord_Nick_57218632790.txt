Artist: Miso Hungry
Album:  Harvest Hues

With a taste for innovation, Miso Hungry crafts sonic landscapes that blend the earthy rhythms of agriculture with the sweetness of discovery. Born from a passion for food science and a knack for experimentation, their sound has evolved through the seasons, much like the edamame they've dedicated their life's work to understanding. From the roots of traditional spectroscopy to the futuristic realms of machine learning, Miso Hungry's music takes you on a journey through the harmonies of harvest and nutrition, always keeping an ear out for the perfect beat of efficiency. With each note, they celebrate the beauty of locally sourced, organic delights and the unsung heroes of the agricultural world.
