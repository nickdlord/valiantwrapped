Artist: Spectra Soul
Album:  Edamame Odyssey: A Frequency Analysis

Meet Spectra Soul, the sonic scientist behind the beats. Born from a fusion of food, chemistry, and technology, his sound is a symphony of flavors and frequencies. With a penchant for crunching data and blending genres, Spectra Soul crafts a unique blend of downtempo electronica and jazzy trip-hop. By day, he's a researcher dissecting the intricacies of edamame; by night, he's a master of sound, weaving together threads of experimentation and innovation. His journey began in the lab, where he delved into the world of spectroscopy and near-infrared reflectance. As he progressed, his fascination with edible delights led him to explore the uncharted territories of food science. The result? A rich tapestry of rhythms and melodies that evoke the essence of his subject matter – a true culinary experience.
