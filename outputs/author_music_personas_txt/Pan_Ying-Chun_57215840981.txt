Artist: VibraSonic
Album:  Frequency of Care

VibraSonic is a sonic maestro who weaves together the frequencies of innovation and artistry. With a background in sonic anatomy, VibraSonic began her journey by splitting sounds and segmenting signals, crafting rhythms from the intricate dance of ultrasonic waves. As her practice evolved, so did her palette, incorporating the resonant tones of dental diagnostics and the gentle hum of rehabilitation. Now, VibraSonic's compositions span the spectrum of sound, oscillating between the precise cadence of clinical imaging and the emotive pulse of human movement. From the whir of cone-beam computed tomography to the soft murmur of constraint-induced movement therapy, every note in VibraSonic's discography is a testament to the beauty of applied science.
