Artist: Ludo Loop
Album:  Variations on a Genome

Meet Ludo Loop, the maestro of molecular harmonies. With fingers poised over the keys of code, Ludo weaves intricate patterns from the threads of genomic DNA. As a virtuoso of variant vibrations, they've spent years perfecting the art of structural refinement – a symphony of segments and splits. But Ludo's repertoire extends far beyond solo performances. In the realm of spatial arrangements, they craft vibrant tapestries of cellular landscapes, revealing hidden geometries within tissue slices. And when the spotlight shines bright, Ludo shines too, illuminating the dark recesses of the human genome with precision and flair. Expect a sonic journey through the realms of precision medicine, where the beat of discovery drives the rhythm of healing.
