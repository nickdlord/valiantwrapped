Artist: Reed Solstice
Album:  Tumors in Harmony

With a soothing blend of sound and precision, Reed Solstice weaves intricate melodies that slice through the noise. A masterful composer of medical frequencies, he began his career experimenting with the resonant tones of intervention, probing the depths of the human body with gentle curiosity. As his artistry matured, so did his focus, coalescing around the subtleties of radio waves and their applications in treating the liver's most insidious invaders. With each new discovery, Solstice refines his sonic palette, blending the rhythmic pulse of innovation with the harmony of healing. His symphonies have been praised for their nuance and emotional depth, a testament to his dedication to pushing the boundaries of what's possible.
