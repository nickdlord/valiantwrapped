Artist: Airwave
Album:  Breathe & Rebuild

Sonic Surgeon Airwave crafts genre-bending soundscapes at the crossroads of innovation and healing. With each note, they dissect and diagnose the nuances of human breath, weaving a tapestry of sonic explorations. Their journey began in the depths of the bronchus, where the rhythm of respiratory rhythms first captivated their ear. As they delved deeper, the intersection of technology and nature sparked a fusion of sound and science. From the crystal clear waters of diagnostic clarity to the turbulent eddies of interstitial lung disease, Airwave's sonic explorations reflect their passion for pushing boundaries and elevating the human experience.
