Artist: Byte-Sized Bandage
Album:  Rebooting Care

By day, she's a data detective, sifting through the noise to find the signal. By night, she crafts sonic cures for the ills of healthcare. Laurie Novak's journey began in the lab, where she mixed metaphors and merged machines, conjuring up innovative solutions to improve the patient experience. As her sound evolved, so did her scope, incorporating AI's sweet harmonies and human-centered design's gentle touch. From EHRs to ethics, Novak's beats blend science and soul, seeking to heal the rift between tech and tender care.
