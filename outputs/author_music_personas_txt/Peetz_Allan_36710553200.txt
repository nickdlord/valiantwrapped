Artist: Fracture & Function
Album:  Split Seconds & Sutured Strings

By day, I'm a doctor, stitching lives back together in the OR. By night, I transform data into melodies that dissect the art of healing. My journey began with a fascination with the machines learning to predict trauma – the dark horse of AI algorithms. But as I delved deeper, I found myself torn between code and conscience. Now, my music reflects the intricate dance between life and death, where every beat is a choice, every note a consideration. From the split-second decisions in resuscitation rooms to the long-term reverberations of care, my soundscapes aim to capture the unspoken symphony of trauma and recovery.
