Ancker's primary areas of expertise include health communication, patient-centered care, and EHRs. She has conducted extensive research on the effective communication of health-related information, including probabilities, risks, and numbers, to patients and the public. Her work has also focused on the development of patient-centered tools and technologies, such as patient-directed decision support systems and patient portals. Additionally, she has explored the impact of EHRs on clinician behavior and patient outcomes, including the effects of EHR usability on clinician burnout and the use of EHRs to predict emergency department return visits.
